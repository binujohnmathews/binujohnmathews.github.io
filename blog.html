Voice Training Notes

1. Build a Stable Vocal Base

Daily warm-ups, breath consistency, and vowel clarity create the foundation for expressive singing. Do not skip fundamental drills even when preparing songs.

2. Train for Expression

Technique and emotion should move together. Phrase shaping, diction, and dynamics are practiced as musical storytelling, not as isolated mechanics.

3. Prepare for Performance

Performance readiness includes breathing plans, confidence routines, and rehearsal discipline. Stage comfort is developed systematically through guided practice.

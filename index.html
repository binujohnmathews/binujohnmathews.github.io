Music Journey

Binu John Mathews is a multi-talented music professional from Kerala known for vocal coaching, live performance, lyric writing, and composition. He has trained playback singers in South India and currently mentors students from six countries, including those following ABRSM, Trinity College London, and London College of Music curricula.

  • Former vocal trainer on Asianet's Idea Star Singer.
  • Vocal trainer on Mazhavil Manorama's Indian Voice.
  • Creator of 30+ Malayalam Christian devotional songs.
  • Specialized in both Carnatic and Western classical methodologies.

Training Services

Every lesson is personalized for voice range, musical goals, and performance confidence. Sessions are available for individual learners, groups, and choirs.

Vocal Training

Voice Culture • Technique • Repertoire

Build range, tone, pitch accuracy, and vocal stamina through structured exercises and song-based practice. Training includes healthy voice use and stage-ready delivery.

Keyboard Lessons

Beginner to Advanced • Theory Integration

Keyboard and piano lessons for all levels with focus on technique, timing, musical expression, and practical performance skills for solo and accompaniment contexts.

Choir Workshops

Choir Direction • Harmony • Stage Craft

Interactive choir workshops on vocal blend, sight-reading, diction, and coordinated performance for churches, schools, and community ensembles.

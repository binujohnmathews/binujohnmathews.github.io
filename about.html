Biography

Binu John Mathews is a music professional from Kerala recognized for his work as a vocal trainer, performer, lyricist, and composer. His teaching journey spans more than two decades across classroom settings, one-to-one coaching, and broadcast music platforms.

He has trained playback singers in the South Indian music industry and currently mentors students from six countries. His students include learners following ABRSM, Trinity College London, and London College of Music pathways.

As a vocal coach, he has contributed to television platforms including Asianet's Idea Star Singer and Mazhavil Manorama's Indian Voice. His repertoire and teaching system integrate Carnatic and Western classical practices with practical stage application.

Teaching Approach

Training is designed to balance technique and expression. Sessions cover breath work, tone development, pitch stability, diction, phrasing, and repertoire interpretation. Students are guided progressively from fundamentals to confident performance.

For choir teams and group settings, the focus expands to harmony discipline, blend, and musical coordination. For solo learners, session plans are customized to voice condition, current level, and personal goals.
